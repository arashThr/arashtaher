# Investment Resources

You need to know how to invest your money. I never learned. Here's a summary of some of the best resources I found to educate myself on the topic.

Most important lesson: The absolute best resource is by far the fact sheet (Faktablad) for each fund. Learn to read it.

My studies were biased toward European and Swedish market.

**Notes on fund selection:**
- Nordnet: not physical replication. Handelsbanken: physical
- Länsförsäkringar Global: developed markets, physical replication
- DNB: MSCI World
- Avoid Handelsbankens funds: ESG constraints
- Avoid AMF: fees, returns are the same
